My Personal Reflection

“Let your light shine before others, that they may see your good deeds and glorify your Father in heaven.” — Matthew 5:16

My Reflection

In today’s digital age, it is easy to get lost in the noise of social media and technology. But as a follower of Christ, I believe that faith should guide every action — even online. Putting “Una ang Ginoo” reminds me that every post, comment, and creation should reflect kindness, honesty, and love.

Being a “Light of the World” means using technology responsibly — to uplift, inspire, and share hope with others. Instead of spreading negativity, I choose to be a source of encouragement, reflecting God’s goodness through my digital presence.

My goal is to create a positive impact, one message at a time. Whether it’s helping a friend, posting something meaningful, or supporting good causes, I will continue to put God first in everything I do — both offline and online.
