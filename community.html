Faith in Action

“Serve one another humbly in love.” — Galatians 5:13

Community Service and Advocacy

Our faith calls us to serve others with love and compassion. As believers, we are called not just to speak about kindness, but to live it out through action. This page highlights initiatives that reflect God’s love through community work and advocacy.

Charity Drive for Families

A school-led donation program that provides food, clothing, and school supplies to families in need. Each contribution is a light that brings hope to others.

Environmental Cleanup

Inspired by the call to care for God’s creation, students and volunteers join hands in cleaning and greening public areas.

Youth Outreach Program

Spreading positivity and sharing God’s Word through youth activities, mentorship, and creative arts workshops.
