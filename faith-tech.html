Faith and Technology

“Go into all the world and preach the gospel to all creation.” — Mark 16:15

Using Technology for God’s Glory

Technology is a gift — a powerful tool that, when used wisely, can spread hope, love, and faith around the world. As Christians, we are called to be responsible digital creators who use our skills not for fame, but for purpose.

Faith-Based Content Creation

Create blogs, videos, and podcasts that share testimonies, scripture, and positive life lessons to inspire others online.

Apps that Inspire

Develop or promote apps that help people pray, read the Bible, or reflect on their faith daily — technology that strengthens the spirit.

Online Communities

Join or create digital spaces where believers can connect, support one another, and share their journey with God.

Faith + Technology = Light

When we combine faith with technology, we turn screens into spaces of encouragement. We turn messages into ministries. Every click, share, and post can become an act of worship if we keep “Una ang Ginoo” at the center of what we do.
